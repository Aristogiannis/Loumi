# Loumi - Privacy-First Unified AI Interface

## The Problem

Today's AI landscape forces users into an impossible choice:

1. **Fragmentation**: ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini each require separate subscriptions, separate accounts, and separate conversation histories. Users waste time switching between platforms to access the best model for each task.

2. **Privacy Concerns**: Every conversation you have with AI providers becomes their data. Your thoughts, strategies, sensitive business discussions, personal matters - all stored on corporate servers with varying retention policies and potential training use.

3. **No Data Sovereignty**: Users have zero control over where their data goes, how long it's retained, or who can access it. For professionals handling client data, this creates compliance nightmares.

## The Privacy Innovation: Three Tiers

### Tier 1: Community (1X pricing)

**"Pay less, share more"**

- Full access to all 9 models
- Data is collected and monetized (fully transparent)
- Dashboard shows exactly what data is shared and with whom
- Never sold to: governments, insurance companies, employers, or advertisers
- Ideal for: casual users who want premium AI at a discount

**Transparency Dashboard**
- See exactly which buyers received your anonymized data
- View the estimated value of your data contribution
- One-click upgrade to stop all sharing instantly

---

### Tier 2: Private (2X pricing)

**"Privacy without complexity"**

- Full access to all 9 models
- All data stored on our servers but **fully anonymized**
- Automatic PII sanitization before any data leaves your device
- Only the rightful user can access their conversations
- Request anonymization prevents AI providers from tracking you
- Ideal for: professionals who want privacy without managing local storage

**How Anonymization Works**
- Your requests are routed through pooled credentials (no user-to-request mapping)
- Personal information is automatically detected and masked
- AI providers see generic, anonymized queries - not your identity
- Timing randomization prevents traffic analysis

---

### Tier 3: Sovereign (3X pricing)

**"Your data never leaves your device"**

- Full access to all 9 models
- **Zero server storage** - all conversations stored locally on your device
- End-to-end encryption with keys only you control
- AI providers receive anonymized, contextless queries
- Complete offline access to your conversation history
- Ideal for: lawyers, healthcare, finance, journalists, anyone handling sensitive data

**Zero-Knowledge Architecture**
- Conversations encrypted before leaving your device
- We literally cannot read your data even if compelled
- Deletion is instant and verifiable
- Perfect for regulated industries requiring data sovereignty

## Unique Privacy Features

### 1. Privacy Audit Trail

Real-time transparency into every piece of data that leaves your device:

- Which AI provider received the request
- Exact token count transmitted
- Any PII detected (and whether it was sanitized)
- Provider's data retention policy
- Timestamp and credential used

**You'll always know exactly what data went where.**

### 2. Intelligent PII Sanitization

Automatic detection and protection of sensitive information:

| Data Type | Detection | Action |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Email addresses | Real-time | Anonymize → [EMAIL_1] |
| Phone numbers | Real-time | Anonymize → [PHONE_1] |
| Social Security Numbers | Real-time | Block transmission |
| Credit card numbers | Real-time | Redact entirely |
| API keys/passwords | Real-time | Block transmission |
| Names (configurable) | Optional | Anonymize → [PERSON_1] |

Responses automatically restore anonymized values for natural reading.

### 3. Provider Trust Scores

Crowdsourced, real-time trust metrics for each AI provider:

- Data retention policies
- Training data usage
- Encryption standards
- Compliance certifications
- Community-reported incidents

**Make informed decisions about which model to use for sensitive queries.**

### 4. Burner Sessions

Temporary, zero-trace conversations:

- Set duration (1 hour, 24 hours, etc.)
- No history saved anywhere (not even locally)
- Fresh credentials for each request
- Auto-destructs when session ends
- Perfect for one-off sensitive queries

### 5. Cross-Model Memory

Seamless context across all providers:

- Start a conversation with Claude
- Continue it with GPT-4
- Reference it from Gemini
- All memory stored according to your privacy tier
- No vendor lock-in on your conversation history
